About Ilocos Sur

Welcome to Ilocos Sur, a province rich in history and natural beauty.

Explore our rich heritage, delicious cuisine, and beautiful beaches.

Restaurants

  • Inapuyan: affordable but signature Ilocano cuisines such as sinigang na lechon.
  • Cafe Uno: This cafe is renowned for its delectable cakes, pasta, and a variety of silog meals that are favored by tourists for breakfast.
  • Angkong Seafood Restaurant: a popular restaurant in Vigan City that serves traditional Chinese cuisine.

Hotels

  • Hotel Luna: Hotel Luna is the first museum hotel in the Philippines.
  • Safari Hotel and Villas by Cocotel : designed for escape and relaxation. Spend some time relaxing at the hotel's pool.
  • Vitalis White Sands: a local fishing village, guests will be reminded of the simple way of life.

Tourist Spots

  • Historical Landmarks: Explore centuries-old churches, forts, and ancestral houses.
  • Natural Wonders: Discover stunning waterfalls, caves,and beaches.
  • Cultural Treasures: Immerse yourself in the vibrant local culture at festivals and museums.
